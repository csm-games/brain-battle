Brain Battle Rules

Objective

Thinking Traps team tries to reduce Brain Health to -100, while Alternative Thoughts team tries to increase it to +100.

How to Play

  1. Play a card from your hand
  2. Choose one of the two moves on your card
  3. Target Brain Health or opponent's cards
  4. Explain the thinking pattern for educational value
  5. Use Event Cards for special effects

Card Types

Thinking Traps: All-or-Nothing, Labeler, Mind Reader, Negative Filter, Should Monster, Personalizer, Fortune Teller

Alternative Thoughts: Middle Path Finder, Growth Mindset, Fact Checker, Balanced Viewer, Flexible Thinker, Reality Checker, Possibility Explorer

Special Rules

Alternative Thoughts are especially effective (+10 damage) against their corresponding Thinking Traps!

Event Cards

Thinking Traps: Social Media Storm, Distraction Overload

Alternative Thoughts: Rest & Recharge, Friend Support
